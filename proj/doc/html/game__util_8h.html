enum MoveType

Represents a move type.

This enum represents a move type. It can be either be:

  • Normal: a simple movimentation in the board.
  • Capture: a movimentation that captures a piece.
  • Castle: a movimentation that castles the king and a rook.
  • En Passant: a movimentation that captures a pawn with the en passant rule.
