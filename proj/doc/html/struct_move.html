Detailed Description

Represents a move.

This struct represents a move. It is composed of an origin, a destination, a type, a piece and a secondary piece.

The origin and destination are the positions of the move.
The type is the type of the move.
The destination is the position of the move.
The piece is the piece that is being moved.
The secondary piece is the piece that is being captured or castled with the move.
