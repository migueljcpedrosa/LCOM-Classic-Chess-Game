\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a move. 

This struct represents a move. It is composed of an origin, a destination, a type, a piece and a secondary piece.

The origin and destination are the positions of the move. ~\newline
The type is the type of the move. ~\newline
The destination is the position of the move. ~\newline
The piece is the piece that is being moved. ~\newline
The secondary piece is the piece that is being captured or castled with the move. ~\newline
